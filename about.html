I'm Vinay Kumar, an enthusiastic Interaction Design student at the Industrial Design Center, Indian Institute of Technology, Bombay. My journey in the design world has kindled a deep passion for humanizing technology through design. I'm committed to crafting user-centered experiences that deeply resonate with people. My design philosophy is rooted in the constructivist ideology, where I strongly believe in design's transformative power. It has the ability to shape and enhance our interactions with the world. I approach my work with a social perspective, understanding the profound impact design can have on society and addressing real-life challenges with innovative solutions.
